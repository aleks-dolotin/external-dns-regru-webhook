1. Get the application URL by running these commands:

  export POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "reg-adapter.fullname" . }} -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
  kubectl port-forward $POD_NAME 8080:8080

2. Metrics endpoint: http://localhost:8080/metrics

3. Health endpoints:
  - /healthz (liveness)
  - /ready (readiness)
